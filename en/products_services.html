Our Services

We do not only provide high quality food products, but also provide integrated services to support our customers in Saudi Arabia with innovative solutions.

1. Import and distribution of food products

We excel in importing luxury food products from all over the world and distributing them to the Saudi market. We have a strong distribution network that ensures that products reach all parts of the Kingdom.

  • 1. Local Distribution: Through our branches located in various cities of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
  • 2. International Shipping: To meet the needs of our customers in remote areas.

2. Wholesale and retail service

  • 1. Wholesale: We offer competitive prices for customers who wish to purchase large quantities of food products.
  • 2. Retail: We offer flexible options for customers who wish to purchase smaller quantities.

3. Customize orders

We offer custom order services to meet our customers' specific needs:

  • 1. Special Quantity Ordering: Customers can order larger quantities or customize products to suit their needs.
  • 2. Custom Packaging: Packaging services that give a luxurious look to products.
  • 3. Sales Support: To help wholesalers and retailers select products that meet market needs.

4. Shipping and Delivery

We provide fast and secure shipping services to all our customers in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia via:

  • 1. Fast Shipping: Ensure products arrive as soon as possible.
  • 2. Cash on Delivery Service: To provide convenient and secure options for the customer.

Who are we?

TBA is a Saudi company specialized in importing and distributing luxury food products. Since its establishment, it has focused on providing high-quality imported food products including luxury coffee and chocolate, in addition to a wide range of sweets, biscuits and international products. Through a comprehensive distribution network that includes branches and warehouses across the Kingdom, we are always keen to meet the needs of the Saudi market and provide the best shopping experience for our customers.

Our vision

To be the leading distributor of luxury food products in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, with international expansion into the confectionery and luxury food products sector.

Our mission

At TBA, we import and distribute high-quality food products that align with the latest trends. Our global partnerships ensure premium products are always available in Saudi Arabia at competitive prices.

How did TPA start?

Since 2014, we have established TPA with one goal: to provide the best to customers in the Kingdom. We work with leading global companies to provide a select range of food products that are characterized by high quality and added value. Our distinguished history and experience in the market has made us a trusted name in the field of importing and distributing luxury food products.

Our goals

Despite the challenges facing global markets in recent years, the Global Brands Summit for Trade has continued to grow and expand, as we have significantly increased the volume of our imports to the Saudi market, and increased our partnerships with leading global brands. This includes expanding our distribution centers and warehouses in several Saudi cities.

Our future vision

We aim to be the leading company in the luxury food sector in the Kingdom, and we aim to expand the distribution of our products to include additional markets in the Middle East and the world.

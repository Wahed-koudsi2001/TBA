Who are we?

TBA is a Saudi company specialized in importing and distributing luxury food products. Since our establishment, we have focused on providing high-quality imported food products including luxury coffee and chocolate, in addition to a wide range of sweets, biscuits and international products. Through a comprehensive distribution network that includes branches and warehouses across the Kingdom, we are always keen to meet the needs of the Saudi market and provide the best shopping experience for our customers.
